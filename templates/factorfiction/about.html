Fact or Fiction - About Page

FACTorFICTION aims to allow all visitors to fight against the onslaught of 'Fake News' stories that so many people sadly believe.

Once registered you will be able to submit news stories you believe could be fake and then other users can investigate and let you know what they think but rating your story as 'Fact' or 'Fiction'.

Think you're good at detecting if a story is real or not? Have a go at our FACTorFICTION mini game! See if you can sort the truth from the lies with just a title, short description and photo.
